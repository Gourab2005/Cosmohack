What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a coding event where developers, designers, and engineers collaborate intensively on software projects over a short, fixed period. Hackathons encourage innovation, creativity, and problem-solving, typically focusing on creating usable software prototypes by the end of the event.

Common phases in a hackathon:

  • Forming teams or working solo
  • Selecting a challenge or problem to solve
  • Building a solution or product within the given timeframe
  • Presenting the final prototype to judges

Domains often featured in hackathons include:

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Blockchain and FinTech
  • Mobile App Development
  • Cybersecurity
  • Web Development
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
